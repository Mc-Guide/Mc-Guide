▶ SECTION 02

3D BUILD IDEAS

🏰
MEDIEVAL CASTLE
⭐⭐⭐ HARD

Use Stone Bricks, Cobblestone and Deepslate. Build 4 corner towers with battlements. Add a drawbridge over water, a throne room, and a great hall. Add mossy/cracked bricks for weathering.

Time Estimate8-12hrs
Stone Brick Deepslate
🌲
TREEHOUSE VILLAGE
⭐⭐ MEDIUM

Build multiple houses connected by rope bridges high in the jungle canopy. Use Jungle Wood, Bamboo planks and Mangrove for color variety. Add hanging lanterns and vines for ambiance.

Time Estimate4-6hrs
Jungle Wood Bamboo
🚀
SPACE STATION
⭐⭐⭐ HARD

Build in the sky using Iron Blocks, Quartz, and Glass panes. Create a circular ring structure with a central hub. Add solar panels from Black Stained Glass and observation decks.

Time Estimate10-15hrs
Iron Block Quartz
🐉
DRAGON STATUE
⭐⭐⭐ HARD

Sculpt a massive dragon from Obsidian and Blackstone. Use the pixel-art body shaping technique — build a cross-section grid and fill each slice. Wings from End Stone Bricks look incredible.

Time Estimate20+ hrs
Obsidian Blackstone
🌊
UNDERWATER CITY
⭐⭐⭐ HARD

Drain an ocean bay and build a domed city with Prismarine and Sea Lanterns. Connect domes with glass corridors. Add farms, housing districts and a throne at the center. Use conduit to breathe.

Time Estimate15-20hrs
Prismarine Sea Lantern
🌋
VOLCANO FORTRESS
⭐⭐ MEDIUM

Shape a mountain into a volcano using Netherrack, Magma Blocks and Lava. Build a fortress base around the caldera. Use Basalt for layers and Orange/Red Terracotta for the lava flows look.

Time Estimate6-10hrs
Netherrack Magma
🏛️
GREEK TEMPLE
⭐ EASY

Use Quartz Pillars, Quartz Blocks and Smooth Quartz slabs for a pristine white look. Build tall columns in rows, a stepped platform, and a triangular pediment roof. Very achievable!

Time Estimate2-4hrs
Quartz Pillars
🗼
MODERN SKYSCRAPER
⭐⭐ MEDIUM

Build a glass-and-concrete tower using Glass, Cyan Concrete and White Concrete. Create floor plates, balconies and a rooftop helipad. Interior floors with carpet and furniture make it feel real.

Time Estimate5-8hrs
Glass Concrete
🌸
JAPANESE PAGODA
⭐⭐ MEDIUM

5-tiered pagoda using Dark Oak, Crimson Wood and Spruce. Each tier gets smaller with an overhanging roof using stair blocks. Add a koi pond, cherry blossom trees and stone lanterns around it.

Time Estimate4-7hrs
Dark Oak Crimson
▶ SECTION 03

BEST FARMS TO BUILD

🌾
AFK IRON GOLEM FARM

Build in a village with 20+ beds and 20+ villagers. Golems spawn automatically, fall into a lava blade and drop Iron Ingots. Produces 300–800 ingots/hour while AFK.

Redstone800/hr
⭐
XP BLAZE FARM

Build a platform around a Nether Fortress blaze spawner. Funnel blazes into a kill chamber you can one-shot. Fastest XP in the game — max-enchant a tool in 5 minutes.

Fast XPNether
🐠
GUARDIAN FARM

Drain an ocean monument and convert it into a guardian farm. Produces Prismarine, Sea Lanterns and massive XP. Takes time to build but produces ~180k items per hour.

PrismarineComplex
🥩
COW/CHICKEN COOKER

Breed cows or chickens above a hopper-connected lava blade. Babies grow up, get cooked instantly and drop cooked meat into chests. Infinite food supply forever.

Cooked MeatPassive
🕸️
SPIDER / STRING FARM

Build on top of a spider spawner in a cave. A water funnel pushes spiders to a kill zone. You get endless String (for bows, wool) and Spider Eyes (for potions).

StringSpider Eye
💎
VILLAGER TRADING HALL

Cure zombie villagers to get maximum discount. Set up rows of Librarians, Farmers, Weaponsmiths and Fletchers. Trade paper/carrots for emeralds; emeralds for enchanted gear. Unlimited diamonds.

EmeraldsBest Farm
🎆
ENDERMAN FARM

Build a large platform in the End at Y=128. Endermen fall from the height and you one-shot them. Insane Ender Pearl drops and XP — best late-game XP farm in the game.

End OnlyTop XP
🌵
CACTUS + BAMBOO FARM

A zero-tick cactus farm using pistons auto-harvests cactus into hoppers. Bamboo auto-breaks itself. Combine both for infinite fuel — cactus for green dye, bamboo for scaffolding and sticks.

FuelZero-Tick
🍄
MUSHROOM / BONE MEAL FARM

Use a Composter with a skeleton farm to generate infinite Bone Meal. Feed it into a flower/crop farm for auto-growing food. Essential for early game food security on any difficulty.

Bone MealCrops
